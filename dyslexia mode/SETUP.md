# 🚀 Quick Setup Guide

## ✅ What You Have

All files are ready! Your extension includes:

- ✅ Fonts uploaded (OpenDyslexic-Regular.otf and OpenDyslexic-Bold.otf)
- ✅ Floating toggle button (appears on every webpage)
- ✅ All code files (manifest.json, content.js, styles.css)

## 📦 Load Extension in Chrome

### Step 1: Open Chrome Extensions Page
1. Open Google Chrome
2. Type in address bar: `chrome://extensions/`
3. Press Enter

### Step 2: Enable Developer Mode
1. Look at the top-right corner
2. Toggle ON the "Developer mode" switch

### Step 3: Load Your Extension
1. Click the "Load unpacked" button (top-left)
2. Navigate to and select this folder: `dyslexia mode`
3. Click "Select" or "Open"

### Step 4: Test It!
1. Visit any website (like google.com or wikipedia.org)
2. Look for the floating purple button in the bottom-right corner
3. Click the toggle button to turn Dyslexia Mode ON/OFF
4. Watch the page transform instantly!

## 🎯 What Happens When You Toggle ON

- All text changes to OpenDyslexic font
- Line spacing increases (2.0)
- Letter spacing increases (0.15em)
- Word spacing increases (0.3em)
- Background becomes cream (#FAF9F6)
- Text becomes charcoal (#333333)
- Yellow reading ruler follows your mouse

## 🔄 What Happens When You Toggle OFF

- Everything reverts to original instantly
- No permanent changes to the website
- Perfect restoration

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

**Extension won't load?**
- Make sure you selected the correct folder
- Check that manifest.json is in the root of the folder

**Fonts not showing?**
- Refresh the webpage after toggling
- Check that .otf files are in the /fonts folder

**Toggle button not appearing?**
- Look in the bottom-right corner of any webpage
- It's a purple button with "Dyslexia Mode" text
- Try refreshing the page

## 📝 Notes

- The floating toggle button appears on every webpage you visit
- Your settings persist across browser sessions
- No toolbar icon needed - just use the floating button!

## ✨ You're Done!

Your extension is ready to use. Visit any website and click the floating toggle button to enable dyslexia-friendly reading!
